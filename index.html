Privacy Policy for TOEFL Reading Coach

Last updated: 12/31/2024

1. Information Collection

TOEFL Reading Coach is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's what you need to know about our data collection practices:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Your test results and progress are stored only on your device

2. Data Usage

The app uses data solely for the following purposes:

  • Tracking your test performance and progress
  • Generating statistics to help you understand your strengths and areas for improvement
  • Providing personalized practice recommendations
  • Managing your notification preferences

3. Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Test results and scores
  • Performance statistics
  • Practice history
  • App settings and preferences

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications:

  • All notifications are processed locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can manage notification settings in the app at any time

5. Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View your statistics and progress at any time
  • Reset all data through the app settings
  • Manage your notification preferences
  • No account creation or registration required

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All data storage is local to the device and under parental control.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated revision date.
